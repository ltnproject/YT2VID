How to use

1
Copy the video link
Go to YouTube (or TikTok, Twitter, Instagram…), open the video you want, then copy the URL from your browser address bar. On mobile: tap Share → Copy link.
2
Paste it here
Click the PASTE button or press Ctrl + V inside the URL box. The link will appear immediately.
3
Choose format & quality
Toggle MP4 (video) or MP3 (audio only). For MP4, pick a quality — 720p is a great default. Use Advanced settings for codec, container, and platform-specific options.
4
Hit Download
Press the red Download button. Y2VID tries several live Cobalt instances automatically — usually resolves in 2–5 seconds.
5
Save your file
A green link appears — click it to open your file. On desktop: right-click → Save as. On mobile: tap and hold → Download.
